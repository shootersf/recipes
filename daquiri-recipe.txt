Ingredients:
 - 2oz your favourite rum
 - 1oz freshly squeezed lime juice
 - 1/2oz 2:1 Rich simple syrup (or 3/4 oz 1:1)
 - pinch of salt
 

Directions:
  1. In a cocktail glass of choice add water and ice to chill or place glass in freezer.
  2. Add all ingredients to cocktail shaker with ice. Add rum last in case of a mistake.
  3. Shake hard for 15 seconds while picturing yourself drinking it soon.
  4. Empty cocktail glass and strain cocktail in.
  5. Consume.
  6. Repeat.
